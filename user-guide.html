The Quadcopter is controlled by a Bluetooth Nintendo Wiimote and the controls are simple. The below picture circles all relevant controls and gives a brief description of what they do.


When pressing Button-B (On the back of the Wiimote) the thrust increases, the engine power levels can be seen on a graph that starts when the Java Application starts.
